iRODS 5: Harder, Better, Faster, Stronger


After more than a decade in development, this new major iRODS release provides new functionality, cleaner interfaces, faster implementations, and more robust behavior in unpredictable environments.

For years, iRODS has provided policy-based data management - a fully programmable filesystem with an ecosystem of libraries and tools. We share some best practices to make the system show what it's up to.
