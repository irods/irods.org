ISC 2018 Workshop

ISC 2018
Frankfurt, Germany
June 28, 2018

Agenda

9:00am - 11:00am - Overview and Training

In the first morning session of the workshop, attendees will gain hands-on experience in generating rules to support various use cases and policy requirements. The concepts of resource hierarchies, policy enforcement points, and the maintenance of an archive will be covered in detail.

11:00am - 11:30am - Coffee Break

11:30am - 1:00pm - Member Presentations

The second morning session will be used to explore data management scenarios from the field and will be conducted by users from iRODS Consortium member institutions.

1:00pm - 2:00pm - Lunch

2:00pm - 4:00pm - Demonstrations

The first afternoon session will be devoted to covering iRODS capabilities and demonstrations of concepts such as multiple distributed databases and taking data to compute and compute to data.

4:00pm - 4:30pm - Coffee Break

4:30pm - 6:00pm - Panel Discussion

The second afternoon session will be a panel discussion which will feature application discussions by current users and a question and answer period during which prospective users can explore their use cases with the panel.
