Transportation in Leuven

  • By Bus
    - Bus travel in Flanders is organized by a company called 'De Lijn'.
    - You can check buses via their website (https://www.delijn.be/en/) or via the app of 'De Lijn'.
    - Tickets can be bought via the De Lijn shop (at the station of Leuven), via the website, via the app or via SMS.
    - More information
  • By Bike
    - Bikes can be rented in Leuven at Fietspunt Leuven for 10 euro/day or 24 euro/week.
    - Their office is located in the big bike shed next to the station of Leuven (to the left, if you exit the station). They are open on weekdays between 7AM and 7PM.
    - Alternatively, bikes can be rented at Leuven Leisure.
