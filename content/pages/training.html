iRODS Training

The iRODS Consortium periodically offers three-day courses to train iRODS System Architects, DataGrid Administrators, iRODS Developers, and iRODS Support Technicians. Specialized courses and workshops are also available on request. Courses will take place at RENCI headquarters in Chapel Hill, NC, or on-site training can be arranged. Cost is per student, with multiple-student and educational and nonprofit discounts available. Contact us for more information.
