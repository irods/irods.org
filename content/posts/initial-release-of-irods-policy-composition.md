The iRODS Consortium is pleased to announce the first release of [iRODS Policy Composition](https://github.com/irods/irods_rule_engine_plugin_policy_composition/tree/0.1.0).

It is released today as v0.1.0 and targets iRODS 5.0.2.

iRODS Policy Composition is designed to simplify policy enforcement by allowing administrators to think in terms of configuration and composability rather than having to write code.

Several rule engine plugins are provided, covering areas such as data movement, data retention, verification, and more.

You can see its early development through these three talks:

- [TRiRODS, Feb 2020](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LP6ywWCTB8A)
- [UGM 2020](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-WrGokRzSug)
- [UGM 2021](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2bgg54DEmnc)

Give it a try. We look forward to your feedback.
